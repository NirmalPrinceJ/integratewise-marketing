What Continuity Means

FOUR DIMENSIONS OF CONTINUITY

Memory Continuity

Knowledge no longer lives inside individual conversations. Decisions, learnings, and organizational intelligence become persistent assets. Every interaction strengthens the same memory foundation.

Context Continuity

Work no longer resets when tools change. Start in ChatGPT. Continue in Claude. Move to OpenWebUI. Transition to an internal agent. The work continues because the context persists.

Reasoning Continuity

Organizations develop ways of thinking, operating, and making decisions. Those patterns survive model changes, vendor changes, employee turnover, and technology shifts. Organizational reasoning compounds over time.

Execution Continuity

Workflows, tasks, approvals, and actions maintain state across systems. The organization continues the work — not the tool. Nothing is lost when you switch platforms.
