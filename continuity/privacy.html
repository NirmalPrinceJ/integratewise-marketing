PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 10, 2026

IntegrateWise, Inc. ("IntegrateWise", "we", "us") operates the Continuity Operating System. This policy explains what data we process and how we protect it. We designed our platform on a simple principle: your organization owns its continuity, and we are its custodian.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email, company, and role when you register or request a briefing.
  • Usage data: Product interactions, logs, and diagnostics to operate and improve the service.
  • Organizational data: Content you connect to your Spine (entities, conversations, decisions) — processed on your behalf and isolated to your tenant.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, secure, and improve the Continuity Operating System.
  • To respond to briefing requests and support inquiries.
  • To meet legal, compliance, and audit obligations.

3. Data Ownership & Isolation

Your organizational data belongs to you. It is isolated to your tenant, never used to train shared models, and never shared across tenants. You may export or delete it at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data only with sub-processors necessary to operate the service (e.g., cloud infrastructure), under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

5. Security

We apply encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest, tenant isolation, role-based access controls, and complete audit logging. See our Security page for details.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. Retention windows vary by plan tier.

8. International Transfers

We offer data residency options (US / EU). Where data crosses borders, we apply appropriate safeguards.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated through the service or via email.

10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at privacy@integratewise.ai.
