   **A**: Yes. Annual plans come with a discount. Book a demo and we'll walk you through annual pricing.

5. **Q**: What's included in the Implementation packages?
   **A**: Workspace setup covers your initial app connections, data mapping, and workspace configuration. Connector workshop is a hands-on session to integrate your specific tools. Custom add-ons cover advanced configurations like custom data flows or enterprise SSO setup.

6. **Q**: What happens to my data if I cancel?
   **A**: Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export everything during that period. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted per our data retention policy.
