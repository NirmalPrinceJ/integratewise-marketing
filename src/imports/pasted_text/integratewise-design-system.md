## Company Description

IntegrateWise is a **Knowledge Workspace empowered by AI and the Spine**.

The **Spine** is a unified intelligence layer that connects tools, knowledge, context, and decisions across systems.

The platform allows AI to **think in context and propose actions**, while **all execution remains governed through human approvals**.

Core product philosophy:

**AI Thinks in Context — and Waits for Approval**

The brand should communicate:

* intelligence
* trust
* structured systems
* connected data
* enterprise-grade reliability
* human-governed AI

The visual tone should feel similar to companies like:

* Stripe
* Linear
* Notion

* Snowflake

Clean, intelligent, modern SaaS design.
