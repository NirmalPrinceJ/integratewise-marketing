## Section 1 — The 2025 Problem

### Headline

**Modern work broke before AI arrived. AI only exposed how broken it already was.**

### Body Copy

By 2025, most organizations are operating across a growing maze of tools, dashboards, spreadsheets, documents, chat systems, email threads, support platforms, and AI interfaces. Work is spread everywhere, but understanding is not.

Teams do not struggle because they lack software. They struggle because the truth is fragmented across too many systems.

Knowledge is scattered across documents and conversations. Operational signals live in disconnected tools. AI generates answers without seeing the full picture. Execution often moves forward without strong governance, traceability, or oversight.

This creates a new kind of operating problem:

* systems are connected poorly or not at all
* context is lost between teams and tools
* AI output is often shallow because it lacks real business understanding
* automation can move faster than governance
* teams spend too much time rebuilding context instead of acting on it

### Closing Line

**Modern work is fragmented. IntegrateWise is designed to reconnect it.**
