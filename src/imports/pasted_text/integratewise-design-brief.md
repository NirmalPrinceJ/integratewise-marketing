### Margins

* Right: 20–24 mm
* Bottom: 18–22 mm

### Header structure

Top right:

* Bengaluru, India
* [connect@integratewise.co](mailto:connect@integratewise.co)
* integratewise.ai

### Recommended hierarchy

* Company name: 13–15 pt, SemiBold
* Descriptor: 8.5–10 pt, Regular
* Contact details: 8–9 pt

### Divider

A thin line below header:

* 1.5–2 px
* blue or subtle blue-to-coral accent

### Body area

Keep very clean. No heavy graphics.

### Footer

Centered or aligned left:
**IntegrateWise LLP | Bengaluru | integratewise.ai | [connect@integratewise.co](mailto:connect@integratewise.co)**

### Watermark

Use the icon-only symbol from the logo at:

* 3–5% opacity
* centered
* large, faint, non-distracting

For premium brand consistency:
