Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with IntegrateWise?

▼

Getting started is simple: Sign up for a free account, connect your first tool (CRM, support desk, etc.), and create your first workflow. Our onboarding team will help you every step of the way. Check out our "Getting Started" guide for detailed instructions.

Can I integrate with custom or legacy systems?

▼

Yes! IntegrateWise supports 1000+ apps out-of-the-box, plus custom API integrations. Our API-first architecture means if your system has an API, we can integrate with it. Contact our sales team to discuss custom integration requirements.

How is my data secured?

▼

We take security seriously. IntegrateWise is SOC 2 Type II compliant, uses end-to-end encryption, and maintains strict data governance policies. See our Security & Compliance Guide for detailed information.

What support do you offer?

▼

We offer 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, and dedicated support for enterprise customers. Our support team can help with setup, troubleshooting, and optimization of your integrations.

Can I use IntegrateWise with my existing tools?

▼

Absolutely! IntegrateWise works with your existing CRM, support desk, billing system, and any other tools you already use. We integrate on top of your current setup without requiring any changes to existing workflows.
