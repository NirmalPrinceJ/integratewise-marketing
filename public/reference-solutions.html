Support tickets are reactive snapshots. By the time a customer opens a critical ticket, the underlying issue has been brewing for weeks. You have no way to connect ticket trends with product usage, customer health, or relationship history.

Reactive Support Model
  • Tickets treated in isolation, no account context

The Outcome
